WHO WE ARE


Established in 1986, Engineering Plastics is a global supplier of precision injection moulded plastic components. As an IATF 16949:2016 (ISO 9001:2015) certified manufacturer, we have delivered high-quality parts for verticals ranging from automotive and electrical, to consumer goods, medical devices, packaging, and recreational goods.

THEY USE PARTS WE’VE MADE


Whether you need a single prototype, or series production for millions of parts, Engineering Plastics’ customized manufacturing services can deliver your parts cost-effectively, where and when you need them.
