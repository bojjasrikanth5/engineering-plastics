Who We Are

As a global supplier of precision injection moulded plastic components, we work with customers of all scales, from large OEMs to small startups. We are IATF 16949:2016 (ISO 9001:2015) certified, and have delivered high-quality parts to customers and end users in countries across the world, including India, Brazil, South Africa, Mexico, and South Korea, to name a few.

Our team is well-versed in the science of manufacturing, and eager to deliver on customer expectations, no matter the challenge.
